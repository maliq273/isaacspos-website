Deployment Protocol

1. Environment Handshake: Your machine must have an active internet connection for the initial license binding.

2. License Handshake: Every 10 minutes, the system performs a silent handshake with the Isaacs Registry to verify your subscription token.

3. Master Registry: On first launch, the SQLite database is generated. Ensure you save a Master Backup to a physical USB drive daily.
