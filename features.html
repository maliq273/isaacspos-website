Operational Specifications

The Functional Matrix.

Sales Journal

A permanent, immutable record of every transaction, void, and refund. Provides 100% auditing transparency for legal and financial protection.

Loyalty Engine

Proprietary reward logic (R200 = 1 point). Automatically detects and excludes package redemptions to prevent revenue loss.

Color Registry

High-precision chemical vault. Store formulas, chemical volumes, and specific application times linked to a client's technical history.

Z-Report Recon

Mandatory daily audit module. Compares the POS "Card Total" against physical terminal slips to ensure zero variance in bank deposits.

Digital Wallet

Client-side virtual bank account. Used to store change or pre-paid funds, ensuring that all salon revenue remains within your operational ecosystem.

SARS Protocol

Automated tax compliance. Generates legal sequential Tax Invoices with hard-coded registration numbers and VAT headers in PDF format.

Package Hub

Manage pre-paid multi-session bundles. Converts sales into future liabilities, ensuring your accounting reflects future service obligations correctly.

Staff Hierarchy

Role-based permission walls. Segregates daily stylists from management overrides, protecting the system from unauthorized overrides.

WhatsApp Bridge

Paperless zero-touch receipt service. Delivers itemized invoices directly to the client's phone upon checkout to verify data accuracy.
